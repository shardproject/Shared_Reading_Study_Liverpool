Shared Reading Liverpool

Join our Shared Reading study

We are looking for young adults (ages 18-25) to take part in a 12-week study on Shared Reading Shared Reading groups come together in a relaxed atmosphere to explore compelling reads. A trained reader leader reads out loud and the group then talks about how the text made them feel. Everyone can share their ideas or memories. Participation is completely optional—everyone is welcome to simply listen and enjoy. and well-being. No experience with Shared Reading is needed. Everyone is welcome!

The study runs from late January to early April 2025. It involves weekly 2-hour sessions at The Reader, in Calderstones Park, Liverpool. You would take part in group reading sessions and complete weekly questionnaires. There will be a focus group interview at the end of the study. Sessions will be audio- and video-recorded, but all data will be transcribed and anonymized. The recordings themselves will not be shared with anyone outside the research group and deleted after the end of the project.

As a thank you, we will give you a £100 voucher at the end of the study. If you're interested in joining us and meet the age criteria, we'd love to have you on board!
