READ WITH US

Join us for a research study on Shared Reading from February to May 2025

We would like to invite you to take part in an exciting 12-week study focused on understanding how participating in a Shared Reading group might affect young adults' sense of well-being.
We are looking for individuals between the ages of 18 and 25 to join us.
The study will run from the end of January to the beginning of April and will involve weekly sessions at The Reader, located in Calderstones Park. Each session will last approximately 2 hours, with specific time slots to be determined.
No prior experience with Shared Reading is necessary, and you don’t need to be an avid reader to participate—everyone is welcome!
During the study, you will take part in group reading sessions, and we will ask you to complete weekly questionnaires and participate in a focus group interview at the end of the study. Please note that sessions will be audio- and video-recorded, but all data collected will be anonymized to ensure your privacy.
As a thank you for your time and contribution, we will offer a £100 gift voucher at the end of the study.
If you're interested and meet the age criteria, we would love to have you on board!
